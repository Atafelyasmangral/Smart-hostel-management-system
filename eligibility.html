Eligibility & Prospectus

Eligibility Criteria

Hostels at Kashmir University Main Campus include MA Hostel and MAK Hostel, which are exclusively for Postgraduate (PG) students.

Students enrolled in 5-year or 3-year courses may apply only in the last 2 years of their program.

Hostel forms must be submitted when officially released by the university. Students are eligible only if they reside at least 90 km away from the university.

Zakura Campus hostel is open to both PG and UG students of either campus and has a 65% eligibility cutoff.

Hostel Admission Process

1. When hostel forms are released, students must submit the online form through the official university portal.
2. A provisional list is released after 20 days showing selected candidates and allotted hostels.
3. Selected students must collect a receipt from the Proctor Office and then obtain a physical form from the respective hostel office.
4. Submit the completed form with:
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Affidavit
  • Student Photograph
  • Father's Photograph
  • Xerox of University ID Card
5. Collect a receipt and pay the hostel fee at J&K Bank (located within the university campus).
