🎓 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for accommodation in any of the girls hostels of Kashmir University:

  • The applicant must be a full-time student of Kashmir University Main Campus.
  • The permanent residence of the applicant should be at least 50 km away from the main campus.

💰 Fee Structure

The total hostel fee is ₹39,600, which includes the following:

All hostels have same fee structure

  • Mess Charges: ₹20,000
  • Room Rent (for 2 years): ₹10,000
  • Welfare Fund: ₹4,400
  • Caution Money (Refundable): ₹2,000

Note Students may need to pay additional charges to the mess account based on their monthly consumption. The monthly mess bill is adjusted against the initial ₹20,000 mess deposit.
