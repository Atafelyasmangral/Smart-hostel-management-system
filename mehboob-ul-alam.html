Eligibility

Accommodation in Mehboob-ul-Ulam (MA) Boys Hostel is open to full-time students of Kashmir University whose permanent residence is at least 70 km away from the main campus. Priority is given to students with outstanding academic records and those involved in university activities. Hostel admission is subject to availability and university guidelines.

Rebate Policy

Rebate in mess charges is allowed only for a maximum of seven days in a month, subject to prior approval by the Rebate Committee. Applications must be submitted in advance and are considered as per hostel rules.
